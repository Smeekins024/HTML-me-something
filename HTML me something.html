Here is my list of the top 10 Kickball teams that I have made an appearance on.

  • Thundercankles
  • They Dead
  • Whispering Eye
  • Pick Your Poison (PYP)
  • St. Lunakicks
  • Alpha Q Up
  • FAFO
  • Suicide Squad
  • Hung and Reckless
  • Steady Mobbin

Thundercankles

Thundercankles was the first time i played kickball during the winter. Sounds like a rough time, but due to it being St. louis winters, we still had a decent mix of snow and good weather games. It was also only my second season playing so i was still learning the game, but also making some lifelong friends in the process.

They Dead

They Dead was my first time being in a draft style kickball league. I was picked in the second round so i felt pretty good about that.

WhisperingEye

Whispering Eye was the first team that I had come to that had already been established. They were all family members so their chemistry was always top notch.

Pick Your Poison (PYP)

Pick Your Poison was the second time i traveled to a different city to play in a national tournament. Amazing Experience and even better people.

St. Lunakicks

St. Lunakicks was the very first team i started playing recreational/competitive kickball for. Summer 2012. The beginning of a legacy. Lol.

Alpha Q Up

Immediately following my incredible run with Whispering Eye, I was recruited to play for Alpha Q Up. Summer 2013.

FAFO

FAFO was a team of players from the St. Louis area, who traveled to Las Vegas for a weekend of fun and games. Found ourselves in the elite 8!

Suicide Squad

Suicide Squad was just a group of friends who decided that playing on sundays wasnt gonna be enough for us so we decided to play saturday as well.

Hung And Reckless

Hung And Reckless was a team full of players that wanted to enter a tournament to see who was the better of the teams out of the missouri and illinois areas.

Steady Mobbin

And last but not least, the team I am currently play for which is a branch of the Alpha Q Up team from 2013, 8 Years and counting, STEADY MOBBIN!!!
